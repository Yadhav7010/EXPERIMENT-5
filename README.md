# Synthesis of Vanadium Pentoxide (V₂O₅) Nanoparticles by Hydrothermal Method

## Aim
To synthesize vanadium pentoxide (V₂O₅) nanoparticles using the hydrothermal method.

---

## Materials Required

### Chemicals
- Ammonium metavanadate (NH₄VO₃)  
- Concentrated nitric acid (HNO₃)  
- Ethanol  
- Double distilled water  

### Apparatus
- Beaker  
- Conical flask  
- Measuring cylinder  
- Burette  
- Thermometer  
- pH paper / pH meter  
- Autoclave  
- Hot air oven  

---

## Procedure

1. **Preparation of Precursor Solution**  
   Dissolve 0.1 M ammonium metavanadate (NH₄VO₃) in 40 ml of ethanol under continuous stirring.

2. **pH Adjustment**  
   Add concentrated nitric acid (HNO₃) dropwise to maintain the pH between 2 and 3.

3. **Color Change Observation**  
   Continue stirring for about 30 minutes until the solution turns yellow.

4. **Hydrothermal Treatment**  
   Transfer the solution into a 100 ml autoclave and heat at 160°C for 24 hours.

5. **Formation of Precipitate**  
   After cooling, a precipitate is formed.

6. **Washing Process**  
   Wash the precipitate several times with double distilled water and ethanol.

7. **Drying and Calcination**  
   Dry the sample and calcine at 500°C for 5 hours to obtain V₂O₅ nanoparticles (orange color).

---

## Result
Vanadium pentoxide (V₂O₅) nanoparticles were successfully synthesized using the hydrothermal method.
